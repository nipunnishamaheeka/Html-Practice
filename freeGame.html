The Mystery Of The Moon



Moon Surface Survival Building Game. Learn to build bases on the lunar surface, maintain survival, explore and discover the secrets inside the moon, and solve anomalies.


Story Background

With the technological advancements in recent years, humanity has achieved lunar colonization. At the same time, a joint scientific research institution has been established, setting up bases on the moon for scientific research. Through controllable nuclear fusion technology, humans have successfully extracted fuel from lunar rocks for nuclear fusion, realizing wireless energy supply and artificial gravity atmosphere, ensuring that the oxygen and pressure around the base remain at near-Earth levels. Due to abnormalities in the moon's operation, human bases on the moon are frequently attacked by unidentified creatures. Currently, all staff related to the moon base have been evacuated, and you have been dispatched as the military's sentinel to investigate these anomalies on the moon and find ways to resolve any discrepancies found.
